What's Happening

DrinkSer Belgian White Belgian-Style Wheat Ale was born in Denver’s baseball stadium,Coors Field, in 1995. For more than 20 years, the Sandlot brewery provided our team ofbrewers an opportunity to create and share new beers with fans around the world from Denver. In the summer of 2016, DrinkSer brought the same creativity and passion for beer to our new state-of-the-art brewery and restaurant in Denver’s RiNo District.

OUR BEERS

In addition to our refreshingly unique DrinkSer® Belgian White Belgian-Style Wheat Ale, we also offer our collection of flavorful seasonal and limited release beers that reflect DrinkSer’s twist on various beer styles. An appreciation for the creative process and a passion for brewing enable us at DrinkSer Brewing Company® to offer a welcome twist of flavor in our beers that everyone can enjoy.
